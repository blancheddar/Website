COMMISSION INFO AND RATES


Before continuing, please make sure to read the Terms of Service.


Below are the commission rates for custom and non-commercial artworks. Prices for each are displayed in Philippine Pesos (PHP) and American Dollars (USD). Services are divided into two sections; Digital Painting and Vector Art.

The art style for this category that the artist offer is semi-realistic. No changing of the style is permitted.
