TERMS OF SERVICE


Thank you for your interest in working with Blancheddar! Kindly read thoroughly the Terms of Service that the artist has prepared for both parties to have a good transaction for the business.



I. GENERAL TERMS


The general Terms of Service apply for Personal Use only.

Commissions intended for Commercial Use will be open for negotiation.

To monitor the feasibility of the project, the Artist requires 3 days’ notice before the project starts.

The Artist will decline time-sensitive commissions that provide an unrealistic timeframe for completion.



II. PAYMENT METHOD


  • A 50% down payment is needed before the project starts. Full payment is acceptable.

  • Payment invoice will be sent via Paypal. (For clients from the Philippines, invoice can be sent via email and further payment options are available.)

  • Strictly no refunds or cancellations once the project starts. However, refunds may be issued if the Client notifies before the project starts. No refunds will be issued after the start of work.

  • If the project is not completed solely due to the Artist’s predicament and or unavailability, a refund may be requested.


III. WORK PROCESS


Once the project starts after the notice, the procedure for a proper transaction is in accordance.


  • The Client should be aware of the art style the Artist provides. The art style is absolute and no changing with the style is permitted.

  • With the Client’s notice, details of information are required in preparation for the project.

  • The 50% down payment is expected to have been paid.

  • The Artist will provide a maximum of 3 major revisions. Major Revisions are only allowed during Phase I of the work process.

  • Please review and be mindful of the commission information.

    • PHASE 1 – PLANNING AND SKETCHING
      • In the first stage, the Artist and the Client are expected to have discussed the Client’s wants and needs.
      • The Artist will be providing an initial sketch.
      • The conditions of having major revisions are present at this stage.
      • Until the sketch is approved, the Artist and the Client will not proceed with the second phase of the project.

    • PHASE 2 – LINE ART
      • Line art is expected to begin at this stage.
      • The conditions of having revisions are presently provided that the details of the change are minuscule (i.e.: bits of erasures, line additions). Major revisions in this stage are closed.

    • PHASE 3 – RENDERING
      • In this stage, the rendering of the artwork will now commence. The Artist will proceed with coloring, shading, and adding intricate details in the process.
      • All minor and major revisions in this stage are closed.

    • PHASE 4 – DELIVERY
      • With the completion of the artwork, full payment is expected to have been completed as well. Any remaining balance will result in the artist having the right to withhold the artwork and or provide the artwork with a watermark.
      • The artwork will be given in HD copy, per the condition of the size and the resolution.

    • Please note that the timeframe for completion relies on the Artist and the Client’s collaboration, as well as the queue and the complexity of the project. Realistically, 1-3 weeks is allotted for the overall process.

    • Process and timeframe may also change depending on circumstances. In a case as such, the Client will be informed as soon as possible.

    • The Artist will update the Client once or twice per week for a progress report.

    • At any point during the commission, If the Client fails to respond to a needed reply within a week (7 days), the commission will be canceled. Once canceled, the partial payment will follow the no refund policy; For full payments, refunds are left to the Artist’s discretion depending on the amount of work already completed.


IV. COPYRIGHT/ USAGE POLICY


  • Upon commission, the Client is purchasing the Artist’s labor only. The Artist retains all rights to the artwork which include but are not limited to distribution, reproduction, promotional rights, etc. (negotiations may be requested.)

  • The Client has the right to claim the character(s) depicted in the commissions, but not the artwork itself. Any promotion of the commissioned piece in the Client’s profiles and platforms must include proper credit toward the Artist.

  • The Client may not distribute, reproduce, or use non-commercial commissioned pieces for profits.

  • The Client may not directly edit, alter, or trace the commissioned artwork itself (e.g.: the Client cannot change the hair color or any features).

  • The Client may not use the artwork or the commissioned piece for NFTs or any platform related to the sale or promotion of cryptocurrency.

  • The Client may not use the artwork or the commissioned piece for Artificial Intelligence (AI) or Machine Learning (ML) training sets, nor process the artwork through generative models.

  • Under no circumstances may the Client submit the artwork for any competitions.


V. ARTIST RIGHTS


  • The Artist has the right to refuse any commission request, without needing to explain the reason.

  • The Artist has the right to decline commissions that go against her standards.

  • The Artist has the right to cancel the commission request or process if the Client establishes poor communication, rude and inappropriate behavior, and or a breach of these Terms of Service.

  • The Artist has the right to display the commissioned piece on any personal website or gallery, and other promotional platforms. Public showcasing may be dismissed or delayed at the request of the client (i.e.: confidential, gift, etc.)

  • The Artist has the right to decline commissions that request sexually explicit materials, degrading, homophobic, and or racist imagery.
